Warranty Period

Lumocast Digital Signage Private Limited warrants its hardware products to be free from manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for one (1) year from the date of product activation. This warranty applies to the original end-user purchaser and is non-transferable.

User Responsibility For Supplied Software

The customer assumes full responsibility for any software, applications, or digital content loaded onto the Lumocast display. Lumocast is not liable for data loss, software compatibility issues, or performance degradation caused by third-party software.

  • Customers must maintain backups of all software and data.
  • Unauthorised OS modifications void the warranty.
  • Software-related support is subject to separate service terms.

Items Not Covered

The following are excluded from warranty coverage:

  • Software misuse and unauthorised modifications.
  • Defects caused by abuse, misuse, or unintended operation.
  • Damage from accidents, neglect, or improper installation.
  • Normal wear and tear, scratches, or cosmetic damage.
  • Damage caused by power surges, lightning, or environmental factors.
  • Products tampered with or repaired by unauthorised parties.
